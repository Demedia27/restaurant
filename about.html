About

Splendid Kitchen was founded in Uyo Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria, by DemsyCoda in December 2019. We specialised in local Nigerian meals for all tribes and culture. We offer outdoor catering service as well. Our food are rich, fresh, clean, and good quality that meets its value.

Our Mission

To serve delicious, high-quality food that exceeds our customers' expectations, in a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes everyone feel at home.

To build a community around food, fostering connections between people, and creating a sense of belonging through shared culinary experiences.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the dining experience, pushing the boundaries of culinary innovation, and setting a new standard for excellence in the industry.

To create a warm and inviting space where people can gather, share stories, and make memories over delicious food and drinks, and to be a beacon of warmth and hospitality in our community

Edikang Soup

Edikang Ikong soup! A classic Nigerian soup made with leafy greens and a variety of ingredients. Edikang Ikong is a popular soup originating from the Efik/Ibibio people of Nigeria, and it's often served at special occasions. The soup is typically made with a combination of vegetables like pumpkin leaves, water leaves.

Jollof Rice

A classic West African dish that never fails to bring people together. Jollof rice is a flavorful one-pot meal made with rice, tomatoes, onions, peppers, spices, and sometimes protein like chicken or beef. The dish is popular in countries like Ghana, Nigeria, Senegal, and Cameroon, each with their own twist on the recipe.

Bolle

Bolle is a special kind of food made with roasted yam, plantain, and potatoes. It is usually served with rosted fish, Kpomo, ]utasi leaf and palm oil stew sauce. This meal has no bounderies in Africa, it cuts across all cultures.
